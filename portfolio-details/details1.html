ABC LEARNING CENTER

ABC Learning Center is an IT Training institute that conducts courses like Programming in Java, Programming in .Net, Microsoft Office, HTML & etc. A website that can be used to provide information about them courses for students and to capture leads generated by digital marketing.

In this individual project, I designed and created the ABC Learning Center website from scratch using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Where HTML is the basis, I build the structure of the page with semantics, ensuring accessibility and continuity of the code. Then, with a touch of CSS, I decorated the site to create a pleasant user experience. I also use Javascript to add interactive elements to the site.

In addition, I provide a place for comprehensive information about each course offered by ABC Learning Center, such as a brief description of the content and benefits of each course, schedule, and price. In addition to providing information about the course, I also created an easily accessible contact page and an online registration form. The enrollment form is carefully designed to make it easy for prospective students to fill in their details and choose the courses they are interested in.
